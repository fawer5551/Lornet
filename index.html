Lornet was founded with a simple question: What if the apps that came with your phone were actually amazing? Default apps haven't evolved in years. We're changing that.

We take the apps you already know - your calculator, your calendar, your clock - and re-engineer them with cutting-edge design, smart features, and the kind of polish usually reserved for premium software. And we're making it all free.
